## 2. math  
It provides mathematical constants, e.g. pi or e, as well as mathematical functions, like log or cos, and also some utilities like ceil and floor.  

## 3. time
- ```time.time()``` 显示从1970/1/1 0点起到现在的时间  
- ```time.sleep()``` 停几秒  
- ```time.localtime()``` 显示当地时间  

## numpy(常简写为np)  
- ```np.array()``` 将列表储存为numpy数组  
> numpy数组仅能储存同一类型的数据，可调用numpy函数计算，计算速度快
